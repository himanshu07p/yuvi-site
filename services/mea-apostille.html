MEA Apostille Service

The MEA Apostille service is designed to help individuals and businesses obtain an Apostille for their documents. An Apostille is a certification that verifies the authenticity of a document for use in foreign countries that are part of the Hague Convention.

What is MEA Apostille?

The Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) in India is responsible for issuing Apostilles. This service is essential for documents that need to be recognized in foreign jurisdictions.

How to Obtain MEA Apostille?

  1. Gather the documents that require Apostille.
  2. Ensure that the documents are verified by the appropriate authorities.
  3. Submit the documents to our office for processing.
  4. We will handle the submission to the MEA and obtain the Apostille.
  5. Receive your documents with the Apostille attached.
